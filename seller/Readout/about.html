History

Originally created by a designer and a developer LAKHVIR SINGH From Swami Premanand Mahavidhalaya, Mukerian (PUNJAB), REUSE-PC has become one of the most popular site in the world for selling and buying new electronics. REUSE-PC was created in Aug-2023 by @LAKHVIR SINGH. A few months into development, site got its first Hack Week and the project exploded as developers of all skill levels jumped in without any external guidance. It served as the style guide for internal tools development at the company for over a year before its public release, and continues to do so today.
