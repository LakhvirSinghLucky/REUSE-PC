Shipping policy

Once your order is placed, it may take up to 2-3 working days to dispatch your order.

Post the parcel is dispatched, shipping takes 1-5 business days or more depending on conditions in your area/city.

You will receive an SMS and email when your order is confirmed and a tracking id (on your email and mobile number) once your order is shipped.

In case a damaged product has reached you, please get in touch with us within 24 hours of delivery.
